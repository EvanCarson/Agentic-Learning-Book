    explanation: "The lesson frames failure as data: a try/except around the tool call is the contract, not defensive programming. The structured error is fed back to the policy like any successful result. Silent swallowing and unhandled exceptions both violate this contract."

    answerIndex: 2
    explanation: "The lesson warns that unbounded retry loops are a leading source of runaway agent cost: the model may confidently retry a call that will never succeed. A small integer budget (two to four attempts) caps this. The budget governs retry count, not tool variety."
